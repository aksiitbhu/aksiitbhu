About Me

Dr. A. K. Singh is an experienced teacher and researcher in computer science. He works as an Associate Professor at Graphic Era Deemed to be University and has been teaching for over ten years. Dr. Singh completed his Ph.D. at the Indian Institute of Technology (BHU), Varanasi, where he focused on social network analysis. Following his Ph.D., he undertook a postdoctoral project titled "PROM: A Data Driven Approach to Understand the Promotion of Violence Across Online Cultures" at King's College London. This project involved analyzing online content to understand how violence is promoted across different online communities, utilizing advanced machine learning and data analysis techniques. His research has been published in well-known conferences and journals, demonstrating his expertise in machine learning and data analytics. Dr. Singh regularly attends international workshops and conferences to stay updated in his field. He is passionate about teaching and research, and he enjoys inspiring his students to learn and explore new ideas in computing.
