Postdoc Project

Title: PROM: A Data Driven Approach to Understand the Promotion of Violence Across Online Cultures

PIs: Dr Guillermo Suarez de Tangil, and Prof. Jose Such

Description: This project investigates the role of alternative online subcultures in the promotion of violence, through a data-driven comparative analysis of content across several online social network platforms.
